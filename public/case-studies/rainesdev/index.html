RainesDev — Case Study

IT Staffing & Recruiting

The Problem

Needed a data-backed business plan, clear GTM, modern website, and digitised operations while maintaining faster, lower-cost placements.

Our Approach

  • Financial modeling and business roadmap across recruitment, marketing, and operations.
  • Defined segments, channels, and messaging; launched nurturing via email and LinkedIn.
  • Website redesign to clarify value proposition and capture inbound leads.
  • Recruitment automation: centralized applications dashboard and SOPs.

The Impact

  • Financial clarity guided hiring and revenue decisions.
  • Stronger GTM foundation and improved outreach focus.
  • Improved digital presence and inbound flow.
  • Reduced manual tracking and faster speed-to-hire.

Key Deliverables

  • Business plan & financial modeling sheets
  • GTM strategy and messaging framework
  • Website redesign & SEO optimization
  • Recruitment workflow automation
