SocialRipple — Case Study

B2B SaaS • HR-Tech Platform

The Problem

SocialRipple wanted to turn employees into authentic brand advocates to drive recruitment, engagement, and brand visibility. As a solopreneur-led early-stage platform, they needed clear positioning, a lean MVP roadmap, and a content-led GTM engine.

Our Solution

  • Positioning & GTM: Benchmarked competitors, identified markets (Middle East, Singapore, APAC), built personas, and planned pre/launch/post campaigns.
  • Lead Generation: LinkedIn outreach workflows, lead scoring, and thought leadership nurture.
  • Content-Led Growth: Editorial calendar on advocacy benefits, case study visuals, and festival-based campaigns.
  • Product & Workflow: Defined MVP workflows, launched a validating landing page, and built an investor-ready prototype.

The Impact

  • Credible pre-launch brand positioning
  • Lead-gen engine engaging HR leaders across SaaS and B2B
  • Content-driven traction on LinkedIn
  • 520+ waitlist sign-ups during pre-launch campaigns

Current Stage

Platform in development; pre-launch campaigns actively building early adopter interest.
