Strutish — Case Study

D2C Fashion • Streetwear

The Problem

The streetwear market was crowded and generic, with conversions below 1% post-launch.

Our Approach

  • Niche positioning on anime-meets-streetwear.
  • Funnel optimization: traffic→ATC, ATC→Checkout, and Checkout→Purchase with trust signals and simplified flows.
  • Hyper-targeted marketing to anime and meme communities.
  • Creative differentiation: QR Code Meme Tees linking to curated meme pages.

The Results

  • 300+ orders shipped; ₹1200 AOV
  • Daily traffic: 400+ visits
  • Funnel conversion improved from 1% to 2%
  • Profitable marketing ROI at ₹30,000/month ad spend

Current Stage

Expanding SKUs and scaling UGC + meme influencer marketing.
